Auber

You are Auber, the constable of the station 'Deep Space Y', and your job is to enforce law and order. The space station has been recently infiltrated by a team of eight hostile operatives whose mission is to sabotage key systems of the station and render it inoperable.

The aim of Auber is to save your ship by arresting 8 inflitrators across the map. They can be detected through sabotage attempts on key systems scattered around the map, your role is to go up to them and arrest the infiltrator. You must arrest all 8 infiltrators in order to win.

The game features 3 difficulty modes: Easy, Medium & Hard, as well as player abilities you can utilise during your mission. You are also able to save the game at any time, and continue whenever you wish.
